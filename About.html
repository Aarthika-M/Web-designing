A Bold Retreat in the Heart of the City

Opening

The Plaza Hotel officially opened on October 1, 1907, designed by architect Henry J. Hardenbergh. It was built as part of a larger plan to enhance the area around Central Park and quickly became a symbol of New York City's rise as a global cultural and financial hub.

Accommodations

Rooms & Suites: The Plaza features 282 guest rooms and suites, each designed with the highest level of luxury. Rooms combine classic elegance with modern comforts, often featuring luxurious fabrics, antique-style furnishings, and stunning views of Central Park or the New York skyline.

Signature Suites: Among the most prestigious suites in the hotel are the Royal Suite and the Plaza Suite, which offer expansive living spaces, private dining rooms, and butler service.

Dining & Amenities

The Palm Court: Known for its famous afternoon tea service, The Palm Court is one of New York's most celebrated dining spots. It offers a menu of classic dishes with a modern twist, set in a beautiful, light-filled room with high ceilings and sweeping windows overlooking the Plazaâ€™s courtyard.

The Plaza Food Hall: This casual dining venue features a variety of food options from gourmet sandwiches to artisanal pastries, all in a stylish, modern setting.

Events & Weddings

The Plaza Hotel is a sought-after venue for weddings, galas, corporate events, and private parties. The hotel's event spaces, such as the Grand Ballroom and the Terrace Room, are designed to host large, sophisticated gatherings.

Weddings: The Plaza is famous for hosting luxurious weddings, with a dedicated wedding team to help curate every detail, from florals to catering to entertainment.

The Plaza Hotel stands as a beacon of luxury, elegance, and New York history. Whether you're staying in one of its beautifully appointed rooms, dining at its renowned restaurants, or simply enjoying a drink at its Champagne Bar, a visit to the Plaza offers an unforgettable New York experience. With a blend of old-world charm and modern amenities, the Plaza continues to be a premier destination for visitors seeking the best that the city has to offer.
